Our Church Story

Christ Church of Eternal Salvation was founded in 1985 with a mission to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and serve our community. What started as a small Bible study group has grown into a vibrant congregation of believers dedicated to worship, fellowship, and service.

We believe in the power of God's Word to transform lives and are committed to creating a welcoming environment where people from all walks of life can encounter God and grow in their faith.
